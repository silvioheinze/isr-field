Export Format

The exported CSV will contain:

  • ID: The unique identifier for each geometry
  • ADRESSE: The address of the geometry
  • GEB_X, GEB_Y: Coordinates (if enabled)
  • Year-prefixed columns: For each year in your data, columns will be created for:
    • USAGE_CODE1, USAGE_CODE2, USAGE_CODE3
    • CAT_INNO, CAT_WERT, CAT_FILI
Example: If you have data for 2016 and 2022, you'll get columns like: 2016_USAGE_CODE1, 2016_CAT_INNO, 2022_USAGE_CODE1, 2022_CAT_INNO, etc.
